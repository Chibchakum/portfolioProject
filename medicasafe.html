    MS 5000 Smart Pill Dispenser

    MedicaSafe 2015-2022

    The MS5000 System looks to solve the opioid crisis by helping patiencts suffering from opioid use disorder regulate and control their treatment dosages. The system allows patients to peceive their treatment refills through the locked, tamper-resistant and tamper-evident disposable cartridges.
    The system consists of three parts:

    • Smart Key which acts as the patient UI and pill dispenser
    • Locked Cartrige which houses the medication
    • Software that tracks and regulates the treatment regiment

    As a team we worked together on the original concept, design, prototypes, manufactured product, and FDA submission + testing.
    My direct contributions included:
    • 3D Design and Rapid Prototyping via FDM and SLA 3D printing
    • Protype Testing and Validation
    • Design for manufacturability
    • Material sourcing and tracking
    • FDA submission and documentation
    • Failure Modes and Effects Analysis on all components of the device
