Projects

Nautilus

Bank Onboarding Tool

The current standard practice to onboard clients to an Investment Bank relies on collecting info through email, PDFs, and Word docs.

Nautilus aims to provide Investment Banks and prospective clients a web based platform to complete all requirements needed to open a new account.

Clients can upload their formation documents, entity information, and regulatory status so that information is available from the beginning.
Investment Bankers can send the list of requirements to client's account where that information can be filled out via forms online.

A document control system will ensure KYC and ISDA documentation can be edited and even negotiated online without sending hundreds of emails.

Heated Gardens

New York City + Iceland

Buildings and industries throw away vast amounts of heat energy and hot water. In NYC, steam used for space heating is cooled using drinking water and dumped into the sewer system. In Iceland, hot water from greenhouses and industry get piped directly into waterways, contributing to thermal pollution.

The heated garden technology captures and uses that extra heat by circulating the hot water through pipes under the soil. The heated soil boosts plant growth in the spring and allows plants to survive over fall and winter.

Buprenorphine Dispenser

MedicaSafe, Inc

Treating opioid use disorder is a major challange for health care providers and patients alike. Medication is given to patients in stanard pill bottles with little to no ability for doctors to track a patient's adherence to their treatment plan.

The MedicaSafe 5000 Smart Dispenser Key and Locked Cartrdige is a product designed and manufactured to carry and track a patient's treatment journey. The pill dispenser unlocks the cartridge and dispenses the correct dose once it is time for the patient to receive their medication.
